Mingfeng Qiu

Postdoctoral Researcher

École Normale Supérieure, CNRS

I am interested in theoretical research on biophysics, soft matter and fluid mechanics. Currently I am working with Francis Corson and Vincent Hakim on developmental biology and morphogenesis.

I obtained my Ph.D. from the Department of Mathematics in University of British Columbia, Vancouver, Canada, supervised by Prof. James J. Feng. I completed my undergraduate training in the Department of Mechanics in Peking University, Beijing, China.

In the past, I have worked on the following research topics:

  • Interfacial dynamics of complex fluids (ferrofluid and liquid crystals).
  • Elastohydrodynamic lubrication in microtextured bearings.
  • Modelling and machine learning controllers for quasi-passive dynamic walking robots.
